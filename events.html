Yearly Events in Leiden

3 October Festival

Every year, the citizens of Leiden celebrate the city's liberation from Spanish rule on October 3rd. This historical event includes parades, traditional foods, concerts, and a lively fairground. The entire city turns festive, with residents and visitors taking part in this cherished tradition.

  • Parades and concerts
  • Traditional herring and white bread
  • Lively fairground and amusement rides

Leiden International Film Festival

One of the most prestigious film festivals in the Netherlands, the Leiden International Film Festival showcases a diverse selection of films from around the world. This festival attracts filmmakers, critics, and cinema enthusiasts, offering a unique platform for both established and emerging talents.

  • Screenings of international and Dutch films
  • Workshops and Q&A sessions
  • Award ceremonies and networking events

Leidse Jazz Week

Every January, Leiden resonates with jazz music during the Leidse Jazz Week. From concerts to jazz club nights, this event gathers jazz lovers and musicians for a week of incredible performances, spreading jazz spirit throughout the city.

  • Concerts by top jazz musicians
  • Jazz club nights and jam sessions
  • Special workshops for jazz enthusiasts
